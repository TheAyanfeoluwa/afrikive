WHAT IS AFRIKIVE?

Afrikive is a digital archive and blog dedicated to preserving and celebrating African stories, histories, and traditions. It goes beyond a typical blog by creating a space for authentic narratives from storytellers, historians, and artists. The platform's mission is to ensure that these stories, from ancient tales to modern-day experiences, are documented and cherished for future generations, thereby preserving a vital part of the continent's collective identity.
